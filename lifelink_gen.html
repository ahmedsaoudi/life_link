LifeLink Generator

Create a personalized alert app for your loved ones in a few simple steps.

Step 1: Choose Notification Service

Select the platform where you want to receive the alerts.

Step 2: Enter Credentials

How to get your Telegram credentials:

  1. Bot Token: Open Telegram and search for @BotFather. Start a chat, send /newbot, and follow the instructions. BotFather will give you a token.
  2. Chat ID: Search for @userinfobot. Start a chat and it will immediately reply with your User ID. This is your Chat ID.

How to get your Pushover credentials:

  1. Log in to Pushover. Your User Key is on the main dashboard.
  2. Scroll down and click "Create an Application/API Token".
  3. Give it a name (e.g., "LifeLink"), and create the application.
  4. Copy the new application's API Token.

How to get your ntfy.sh Topic URL:

  1. Go to ntfy.sh and subscribe to a topic name you invent.
  2. Your topic name must be hard to guess. Use a long, random string.
  3. Your **Topic URL** is `https://ntfy.sh/your-secret-topic-name`.

Your App Is Ready!

Give the generated file a simple name like lifelink.html and share its secret URL. Test it first!
