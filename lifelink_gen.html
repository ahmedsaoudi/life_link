LifeLink Generator

Create a personalized alert app for your loved ones in a few simple steps.

Step 1: Choose Notification Service

Select the platform where you want to receive the alerts.

Step 2: Enter Credentials

How to get your Telegram credentials:

  1. Bot Token: Open Telegram and search for @BotFather. Start a chat, send /newbot, and follow the instructions. BotFather will give you a token.
  2. Chat ID: Search for @userinfobot. Start a chat and it will immediately reply with your User ID. This is your Chat ID.

How to get your Slack Webhook URL:

  1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and click "Create New App".
  2. Choose "From scratch", name your app, and select your workspace.
  3. In your app's settings, go to "Incoming Webhooks" and activate them.
  4. Click "Add New Webhook to Workspace", choose a channel, and click "Allow".
  5. Copy the generated Webhook URL. It starts with https://hooks.slack.com/services/...

Your App Is Ready!

Give the generated file a simple name like lifelink.html and share its secret URL with your loved one. Test it first!
